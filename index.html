About Class

This class is about my favorite field of biology - evolutionary development, often nicknamed Evo-Devo.

About Me

My name is Ciera Martinez. My PhD work was on the evolution of plant shape. I focused on leaf shape, but have also published work exploring the evolution of wood, flowers, roots, and stems. I am now a Post-doctoral Fellow at UC Berkeley studying the evolution and patterning of Drosophila embryogenesis.
